### Understanding the Straw Man Fallacy

**What is it?**
A Straw Man fallacy occurs when someone misrepresents an opponent's argument to make it easier to attack.

**How to Study:**
1. Read the definition provided in the First Degree section
2. Look for real-world examples:
   - News articles
   - Social media debates
   - Political discussions
3. Document at least 3 examples in your Grimoire
4. Write why each example is a Straw Man fallacy

**Practice Exercise:**
- Find one example of the Straw Man fallacy this week
- Explain in your own words what the original argument was
- Describe how it was misrepresented
- Note why this fallacy is harmful to productive discussion
